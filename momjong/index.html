How to Play

Goal: Remove all tiles from the board by finding matching pairs.

Rules:

  • You can only select a tile if it is "Free".
  • A tile is Free if there is no tile covering it, AND it is open on either its left or right side.
  • To help you, blocked tiles are naturally darkened. Bright tiles are ready to play!
